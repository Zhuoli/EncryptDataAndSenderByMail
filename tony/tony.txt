Arjen Robben  is a Dutch footballer who plays for the German Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and the Netherlands national team. Robben has appeared at Euro 2004, the 2006 World Cup, Euro 2008, the 2010 World Cup, Euro 2012 and the 2014 World Cup. Though often classified as a forward, he usually plays behind the forwards as a winger. Robben is known for his dribbling skills, speed, crossing ability and his accurate left foot long-range shots from the right wing.
